This folder contains experiment notes, redacted research files, and draft specifications related to the Energetic Paradigm.

## Purpose

The goal of this directory is to explore whether energy-based, constraint-aware, and coherence-oriented interpretations of AI systems can be made clearer, testable, and operational.

These materials are research documents, not product documentation. Some files are conceptual experiment drafts. Some are cross-platform summaries. Some focus on specific technical directions such as Ramsey-number search, scaling interpretation, and structural constraint formulations.

## Notes

Some filenames are intentionally long or partially redacted to preserve source traceability while limiting unnecessary disclosure.

This folder will continue to evolve as experiment designs become more formal, more reproducible, and more closely tied to measurable claims.

## Status

The current contents are primarily research drafts and redacted working documents. They should be read as exploratory materials rather than finalized empirical results.
